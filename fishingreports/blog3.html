Orlando Fishing Trip

POSTED ON July 6, 2016 BY FLATADDICTED


Orlando fishing trips and guided fishing tours near orlando have been on a rise over the past few years. Vacationing families from around the globe have discovered phenomenal fishing just a short 45 minutes from the Disney World Resorts. When anglers are visiting Orlando and discover we have world class saltwater fishing this close to the theme parks they immediately call us to book a trip. The best inshore flats fishing occurs in the Mosquito Lagoon near the Kennedy Space center, Titusville FL.

Inshore Fishing Report

Red fishing in the Mosquito Lagoon has been excellent as of lately. The shallow flats are producing smaller catches of Redfish from 2 to 8 pounds and Sea Trout in the 3 to 9 pound range. Out on the deeper flats and along the edges of sandbars huge Redfish in the 15 to 30 pound range show up mid morning and give us a shot just feet from the bow of boat. This year has been exceptionally hot weather and the bite has been best during the cooler parts of the day, like the first few hours of the morning between 6 am and 10 am.

Inshore Fishing Report

The nearshore fishing outside of Port Canaveral and Cape Canaveral Florida has been producing Tarpon near the beach and along the Canaveral Bight. Bonita are schooled up in huge groups and hitting any thing from Fly to live bait. Triple tail are plentiful as well and most days the big ones are possible targets. Cape Canaveral is famous for those huge Triple Tail.
